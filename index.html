Steamboat Springs Nativity Open House

The Steamboat Springs chapel of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints is throwing a Christmas party for the community!

The chapel is hosting a community creche display from 5-8PM on Saturday, December 9th.

  • Where: The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints, Steamboat Springs
  • When: Saturday, December 9th, 5-8PM

Yampa Valley residents are invited to view an assortment of Nativity creche scenes, listen to Christmas music, and enjoy appetizers and desserts. The event is free and open to the public. It is an open-house type evening, where visitors are welcome to come and go throughout the evening. Attendees are asked to bring nonperishable food items to benefit LIFT-UP. Anyone who would like to include their Nativity scenes or other holiday displays is welcome to bring them to the LDS church on Friday evening between 6 and 8 p.m.

Schedule:


5PM: Party starts. Come in and see a Christmas message (which we'll be playing througout the night), view community nativity scenes, and mingle.
7PM: Our "Special Guest" arrives. (Don't tell the kids - it's Santa!)
7:30PM: Join us for some carolling around the piano. We'll provide the music, you provide your beautiful voice!

If you'd like to bring a nativity scene to the party or you just have questions, please fill out the form below and we'll get back to you as quickly as possible.
