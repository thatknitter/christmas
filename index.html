Steamboat Springs Nativity Open House

It's the most wonderful party of the year! That's right, it's time for the annual Christmas party! Come share our party with us! We'd love if members of the community could show off their favorite nativity scenes, eat some delicious food, and mingle for an evening of seasonal cheer.

  • Where: The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints, Steamboat Springs
  • When: Saturday, December 9th, 5-8PM

Schedule:


5PM: Party starts. Come in and see a Christmas message (which we'll be playing througout the night), view community nativity scenes, and mingle.
6PM: Our "Special Guest" arrives. (Don't tell the kids - it's Santa!)
7PM: Join us for some carolling around the piano. We'll provide the music, you provide your beautiful voice!
